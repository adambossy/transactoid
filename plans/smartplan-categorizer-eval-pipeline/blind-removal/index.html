Scope removal

Blind removal

Delete the blind eval mechanism entirely — 7 call sites plus helpers — keeping _normalize_descriptor.

Requirements

  1. The codebase no longer carries an evaluation mechanism we've decided not to use.
  2. Nothing outside the evaluation tooling breaks when the blind feature is removed.

Why blind goes away

The old "blind" mechanism hid a merchant's own history from the agent so it couldn't echo its prior categorization. With a legacy-vs-agent A/B plus human adjudication, that signal is no longer needed — the human is the ground truth, and we want the agent to use history exactly as it does in production. Blind is dead weight and is removed as part of this change.
