Open questions

  • Will the continuity gate test pass? The single-store flip (persist_session=False) is conditional on it. If it can't be made green, the flip is skipped and Penny double-persists for now. Depends on Model continuity / commit 6.
  • Does agent-ui require explicit step markers at hydration? The plan re-synthesizes them from message/role boundaries rather than persisting them; if agent-ui needs richer step structure, that synthesis grows. Depends on Hydration.
  • When does a single part outgrow TOAST? Per-part byte caps are deferred until a part (e.g. inline base64 chart images from generate_chart) routinely exceeds the TOAST sweet spot. The trigger threshold is unmeasured. Depends on Migration and Risks.
  • When is alembic adopted? Named as "the first non-additive schema change" (e.g. adding user_id after data exists) but not yet scheduled. Depends on Migration.
